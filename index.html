About Jane

I learned this the hard way,
so you don't have to.

I'm not an accountant or financial advisor — I'm someone who figured out bookkeeping and financial organization as an entrepreneur, then spent years helping businesses and nonprofits do the same.

I started my career at a bank, working as an account manager and earning my securities license. But it was running my own business that taught me what really matters: having a clear, simple system you actually understand and use.

Since then I've done bookkeeping for small businesses and nonprofits, set up QuickBooks systems from scratch, and helped people dig out from years of catch-up work. I built the My Money Organized spreadsheet tool because I wanted something that worked the way real people think about money.

My goal is always to work myself out of a job. The sooner you're running your own books with confidence, the better I've done mine.
